Latitude and Temperature



Situation


Does temperature decrease on earth as one moves further away from the equator? Although the question may invoke a 'duh' response, how can it be proven?

Objective


The objective was to prove that in general temperatures decrease on earth as one moves further from the equator.

Method


To test our hypothesis we:
  1. Selected a random point on the earth
  2. Generated a set of uniformly distributed pairs of latitudes and longitudes
  3. Selected the nearest city in the area around each point, if any
  4. Obtained current wether data for each of the over 500 cities for comparison
    • Temperature
    • Humidity
    • Wind Speed
    • Cloud Cover
  5. Conducted a statistical analsys of the results

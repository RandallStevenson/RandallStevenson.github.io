Latitude vs. Cloud Cover



Description & Significance



Like humidity there is a slight trend toward increasing cloud cover as one goes to the north
in both the southern and northern hemispheres. The corrrelation between cloud cover and latitude is
negligible at 0.025.

Note: The concentration of plots at cloud cover percentages 20%, 40%, 75% and 90%
are indicative of the lack of precision in measuring cloud cover.

Description & Significance


Although it may not be apparent from visual inspection, wind speeds tend to increase as one gets further from the equator.
The correlation coefficient between wind and absolute value of latitude in our data was 0.207.
The wind speeds are significantly affected by temperature gradients, since hotter air rises and colder air drops.
The greatest gradients occur further from the equator, producing stronger winds.
